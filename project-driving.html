AUTONOMOUS DRIVING

At Lifewood, we help shape the future of transportation by supporting the AI behind self-driving cars. Our data teams work with global innovators to train autonomous vehicles to see, think, and move on their own.

Test the Sensors: See How AI Perceives the World

Each sensor provides a unique perspective crucial for autonomous intelligence. Lifewood's data fuels their precise understanding.

Camera

Camera sensors rely heavily on image recognition. Lifewood provides the highly annotated images (bounding boxes, semantic segmentation) these models learn from, enhancing object detection and scene understanding for safer driving.

LiDAR

LiDAR provides incredibly precise 3D point cloud data. Lifewood labels these point clouds (3D cuboids, object tracking) to train AI for accurate spatial awareness and robust obstacle avoidance, even in complex environments.

Radar

Radar excels in detecting distance and velocity, crucial for predicting movement and avoiding collisions. Lifewood helps correlate raw radar signals with ground truth, essential for training AI to track objects reliably and make informed decisions in all weather.

Ultrasonic

Ultrasonic sensors are primarily used for short-range obstacle detection, common in parking assistance and low-speed maneuvers. Lifewood aids in validating and annotating data for fine-tuned proximity sensing and automated parking features, ensuring precision in tight spots.

AI Data Extraction for Automotive Leader

Intelligent Capture Redefined

The Challenge

A leading automotive technology company needed to accelerate the training of their autonomous driving perception models. Their existing process for annotating millions of video frames was slow, costly, and prone to human error, creating a significant bottleneck in their development pipeline.

The Solution

Lifewood deployed a specialized team of over 200 data annotation experts working in a 24/7 cycle. We established a secure, high-throughput pipeline utilizing a combination of proprietary and industry-leading tools to perform semantic segmentation, bounding box annotation, and lidar point cloud labeling. Our multi-layered quality assurance process, involving both automated checks and peer review, ensured data accuracy exceeded the 99.5% target.
